Case No. 3:23-cv-08912-SK

Plaintiff’s Motion to Extend Discovery Deadline

TO THE COURT, ALL PARTIES, AND THEIR ATTORNEYS OF RECORD:

Pursuant to Federal Rule of Civil Procedure 6(b)(1)(A), Plaintiff John Doe hereby moves this Court for an order extending the discovery deadline in the above-captioned matter by forty-five (45) days, from November 15, 2023, to December 30, 2023.

This request is made on the grounds that good cause exists for the extension. Despite the parties' diligent efforts to meet the current schedule, several key depositions remain outstanding due to unforeseen scheduling conflicts among principal witnesses and the recent production of over 10,000 pages of electronic documents by Defendant TechCorp.

The parties have conferred in good faith regarding this request. Defendant TechCorp does not oppose this motion, provided that all subsequent pretrial deadlines are adjusted accordingly to maintain the current trial date in March 2024.

Plaintiff respectfully requests that the Court find good cause and grant this motion to ensure a complete and fair discovery process.
