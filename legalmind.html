Northern District of California

Plaintiff,

v.

MERIDIAN CORPORATION,

Plaintiff's Motion to Strike Section 8.3 of the Master Services Agreement
as Procedurally and Substantively Unconscionable

I. Introduction

Plaintiff Chen Consulting Group, Inc. ("Chen") respectfully moves this Court to strike Section 8.3 of the Master Services Agreement ("MSA") dated March 14, 2023, on the grounds that it is both procedurally and substantively unconscionable under California law.

As established by the deposition testimony of Jonathan Smith, the unlimited liability clause at issue was flagged by Plaintiff prior to execution. Meridian's General Counsel, Mr. Harlow, declined to negotiate the provision, characterizing it as "standard boilerplate" despite its extraordinarily one-sided nature.

II. Statement of Facts

On March 14, 2023, representatives of Chen Consulting and Meridian Corporation met at Meridian's offices in San Francisco to execute the MSA. Present were Jonathan Smith as a witness, Margaret Chen on behalf of Plaintiff, and Robert Harlow as Meridian's General Counsel. Prior to signing, Ms. Chen raised specific objections to the unlimited liability provision contained in Section 8.3.
